# Policy Pack (Minimum Viable Set)

## 1. Access Control Policy

- Unique user accounts required (no shared accounts).

- MFA required for VPN and cloud/SaaS access.

- Access granted based on role (least privilege).

- Strong password requirements enforced.

- Access rights reviewed periodically.

## 2. Backup & Recovery Policy

- Daily backups of critical systems and patient data.

- Backups stored securely (local NAS and offsite/cloud).

- Backup restoration tests performed regularly.

- Backup access restricted to authorized IT staff only.
## 3. Incident Response Policy

- All suspected security incidents must be reported to IT immediately.

- IT team isolates affected systems and investigates the incident.

- Logs and evidence preserved for analysis.

- Management informed of significant incidents and service disruption.
## 4. Vendor Security Policy

- Vendors must comply with security and data protection requirements.

- Vendor system access limited to required functions only.

- Contracts must include data protection and confidentiality clauses.

- Vendor access reviewed periodically.
## 5. Acceptable Use Policy

- Restrict USB ports / external media
- Users must protect passwords and not share accounts.
- Installation of unauthorized software is prohibited.
- Suspicious emails or activity must be reported to IT.
