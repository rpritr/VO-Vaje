# Incident Scenario — Ransomware + Data Leak

On Monday 09.09.2024 at 07:30 AM:
- Reception reports that 5 PCs display a ransom note.
- Shared drive inaccessible.
- One doctor reports suspicious login notification from abroad.
- Local newspaper contacts management asking about alleged data leak.

Assume:
- Backups exist but restore was never tested.
- Telemedicine platform still operational.
- No formal incident response team defined.

Tasks:
1. Classify incident severity.
2. Immediate containment actions.
3. Internal and external communication plan.
4. Regulatory obligations (e.g., GDPR notification).
5. Short-term recovery priorities.
